# Ealani Example Sites

Real Arabic landing-page example websites for Saudi/GCC local businesses.

Important positioning: these are **غير رسمية / speculative design studies** based on public business information. They are examples of اعلاني work quality and conversion thinking; they do not claim the listed businesses are clients.

## Current examples

- `/filter-car-services/` — Filter Car Services & Wash, auto repair / car wash, Jeddah.
- `/touch-levantine-salon/` — Touch Levantine Salon, hair salon, Jeddah.
- `/black-stores/` — المخازن السوداء | black stores, electronics store, Jeddah.

## Source facts used

Only public/basic facts supplied by the founder from Google Maps were used: business name, category, rating/review count, and address. Sales copy beyond those facts is written as conversion design copy, not as verified claims from the business.
